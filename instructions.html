Technical Analysis¶

CodeIT Suisse 2019

Problem Statement¶

Use the first few known values of a stock price as training data to recommend buy and sell trades for the next few unknown values of test data.

Unfortunately, training data values are truncated to integers.

On a bright side, you know function family that forms the price on the full index range (training data immediately followed by test data):

\begin{equation} price_t = base + trend * t + \sum_{i=1}^{sine\_count} scale_i * \sin(2\pi * period\_count_i * t) \end{equation}

where

\begin{align} t \in [0, 1]\>evenly\>spaced\>on\>the\>full\>index\>range \end{align}

Scoring¶

Input data contains 4 scenarios in this order:

Scenario sine_count weight
1 1 0.1
2 2 0.2
3 3 0.3
4 4 0.4

Assuming the initial amount of cash is 1, scenario score is: \begin{equation} score_i = \max(0, \dfrac{final\_cash_i - 1}{max\_final\_cash_i - 1}) \end{equation}

where

  • final_cash - final amount of cash after trading on your recommendations
  • max_final_cash - final amount of cash after trading on optimal strategy a.k.a. "buy low, sell high"

Total score is a ceiling of a weighted sum of the scenario scores: \begin{align} total\_coorinator\_score = \lceil{100 * \sum_{i=1}^{scenario\_count} weight_i * score_i}\rceil \end{align}

Input¶

List of 4 training data sequences, each containing 100 values indexed [0, 99]

Boundaries: \begin{align*} \\&base \in [200, 300) \\&trend \in [-100, 100) \\&scale_i \in [5, 15) \\&period\_count_i \in [10, 100) \\&price_t > 0 \end{align*}

Output¶

List of trade recommendations for each test data sequence. Every test data sequence contains 1,000 values indexed [100, 1099] hence every trade recommendation is assumed to be a sequence of alternating buy-sell indexes, monotonically increasing and contained within [100, 1099]

Rules¶

Initial amount of cash is 1 and the first trade is always a "buy".

Every trade is full, i.e.:

  • buy converts all cash to stock
  • sell converts all stock back to cash

If the last recommended trade is a "buy" (i.e. recommendation contains odd number of indexes), then a sell will be implied at the index 1099.

Solution timeout is 28 seconds.
